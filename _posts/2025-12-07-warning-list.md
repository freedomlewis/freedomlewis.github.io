# Principles and disciplines

## Take more time for huge decision, the more impact the more time spent on it.

Job decision must take 2 ~ 3 months to think!

Impact more than 50 thousands cost, must have at least 2 weeks to thinking!

Impact more than 10 thousands cost, must have at least 1 week to thinking!


## Take a decision tree, list the cons and pros first, and considering for a while, no rush to make decision.

## Take more time to investigate and get as much data as possible before make the decision
